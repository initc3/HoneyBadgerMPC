honeybadgermpc.triple_refinement.refineTriples(context, a_dirty, b_dirty, c_dirty)[source]¶

This method takes dirty triples and refines them.

Arguments:
context {PassiveMpc} – MPC context. a_dirty {list[Share]} – Shares of first part of the triples. b_dirty {list[Share]} – Shares of second part of the triples. c_dirty {list[Share]} – Shares of first*second part of the triples.
Returns:
list[Share] – Shares of first part of the refined triples. list[Share] – Shares of second part of the refined triples. list[Share] – Shares of first*second part of the refined triples.
